BANQ is I and my teammates' entry for the EOSIO Virtual Hackathon, Coding for Change. We developed a decentralized application, for running fundraisers, on the the EOSIO bloackchain. In the fundraising economy, concerns regarding how funds raised are being utilised are continuously growing and to address that, we came up with BANQ, a decentralized donations bank for running fundraisers. BANQ takes advantage of the Blockchain technology's security and transparency guarantees to enable secure transactions on the application via an EOSIO smart contract. I was primarily in charge of the smart contract which was written in C++17.
